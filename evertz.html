    Evertz Microsystems is a Canadian devloper and manufacturer of broadcasting equipment. Their main headquarters is based in Burlington, Ontario with other offices in the United States, United Kingdom, Germany, United Arab Emirates, India, Hong Kong, China, Singapore, and Australia. They currently employ over 2000 people at their head office. Evertz specializes in delivering end to end broadcasting solutions with systems for content creation to distrobution. They currently deliver content to televevision, on demand services, IPTV and mobile.

    In 2016 Evertz recieved an Emmy for their DreamCatcher Replay and production suite.
