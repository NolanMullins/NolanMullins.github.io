Company

CAScination AG is a swiss medical technology company located in Bern, Switzerland. They're dedicated to the development of computer-assisted and image-guided surgery. Their navigation systems and surgical robots are designed to improve outcomes for patients undergoing surgical or interventional procedures and offer new perspectives to patients worldwide. Their CAS-One planning and navigation system is used to treat patients suffering from soft tissue cancer by many hospitals across Europe.

My Job

While working at Cascination I mainly focused on Ultrasound navigation to aid cancer ablation needles. My main task was to overhaul the user interface of the system in order to improve the usability of the system. To do this we completely gutted the front end Qt and stripped out the view logic. While doing this one of the most important aspects of the project was to deliver a rock solid solution as we cannot afford to have a software issue during a surgery. We used extensive unit testing and integrated them with our build system (Jenkins) to verify each pull request before merging into master.
